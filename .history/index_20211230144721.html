Our goals

BookPod team want our unique collections of books and podcasts to be available when, where and how users need them. We make One of our goals is to inspire learning, research and discovery by bringing together knowledge, information and people. And make it free for evryone. We want you to be able not only to read the books, but also to hear the authors of these books.
